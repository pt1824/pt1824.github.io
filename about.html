About Us

At Bedrock Boulders, we specialize in creating custom headstones, ranch signs, and commercial signage that are as strong and enduring as the stories they represent.

We proudly serve the greater Houston area, offering families and businesses a better, more affordable alternative to traditional granite. Using engineered stone, we deliver personalized, lasting products—crafted with care and completed in as little as 6 to 8 weeks.

Our team understands how meaningful every project is—whether it’s marking a loved one’s resting place, identifying a ranch, or branding your business. That’s why we combine quality craftsmanship, fast turnaround, and honest pricing in everything we do.

  • Custom Designs – Tailored to your needs, whether memorial or marketing
  • Locally Owned – Houston-based, Texas proud

  • Friendly Help – We walk you through every step with care and clarity
