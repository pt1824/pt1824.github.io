About Bedrock Boulders

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Bedrock Boulders was created to make high-quality, custom stonework accessible to everyone. Based in Houston, Texas, we specialize in crafting custom headstones, ranch signs, and commercial signage using durable, weather-resistant engineered stone. Our vision was born from a desire to offer affordable alternatives to traditional stone and granite, ensuring families, ranchers, and businesses can honor their legacies without excessive costs or delays.

Our team--all Houston-born and bred natives--saw an opportunity to bring our craftsmanship and expertise to a larger audience at an affordable price while still maintaining exceptional quality. In a very short time, we have gained a sterling reputation for affordability, speed, excellence and personalized service.

Our Commitment to You

At Bedrock Boulders, every project is personal. We understand the emotional weight of commemorating a loved one or marking a significant place. That’s why we guide you through every step with care and respect. Our process includes:

  • Personalized Consultation: We listen to your vision and needs.
  • Custom Design: Tailored designs that reflect your story.
  • Expert Craftsmanship: Precision work in our Houston workshop.
  • Seamless Installation: Professional delivery and setup.
  • Ongoing Support: Available for maintenance or future projects.

As a locally owned business, we’re proud to give back to Houston through partnerships with local charities and veteran organizations, strengthening the community we serve.
