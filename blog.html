How to Maintain Your Headstone for Long-Lasting Beauty

Posted on May 10, 2025 | By Bedrock Boulders Team

A headstone is more than a marker—it’s a lasting tribute to a loved one’s legacy. Proper maintenance ensures it remains beautiful for generations. At Bedrock Boulders, our engineered stone headstones are designed for minimal upkeep, but a few simple steps can keep them looking pristine.

Start by cleaning gently with a soft brush and water to remove dirt or debris; avoid harsh chemicals that could damage the finish. For stubborn stains, a mild, pH-neutral cleaner is safe for engineered stone. Check for signs of wear annually, especially after heavy storms, to ensure the headstone remains stable. Unlike natural stone, our engineered stone resists weathering, cracking, and fading, making it ideal for Houston’s humid environment.

Additionally, consider the surrounding area—trim grass or plants to prevent overgrowth that could obscure the memorial. If your headstone includes engravings, ensure they remain legible by cleaning them carefully.

At Bedrock Boulders, we provide guidance on care specific to our materials, ensuring your memorial stays vibrant with little effort. Want to learn more about preserving your headstone? Contact us for expert tips tailored to your memorial’s design.

Navigating Cemetery Regulations for Your Custom Headstone

Posted on March 1, 2025 | By Bedrock Boulders Team

Designing a custom headstone involves more than aesthetics—cemetery regulations play a critical role. At Bedrock Boulders, we help families in Houston navigate these rules to create compliant, meaningful memorials.

Regulations vary by cemetery, often specifying size, material, and placement. For example, some cemeteries limit headstone height or width to maintain uniformity, while others restrict materials to ensure durability. Our engineered stone meets most cemetery standards, offering weather resistance and versatility for intricate designs. We also consider installation requirements, ensuring the headstone is securely placed to withstand Houston’s storms.

Before starting, check with your cemetery for specific guidelines—some may require permits or pre-approval for engravings. Our team assists by coordinating with cemetery staff to confirm compliance, saving you time and stress. We also provide flexible design options to work within restrictions while honoring your vision, from simple markers to elaborate monuments.
