Where Durability and Design Come Together in Stone

Choosing Boulder Designs by Bedrock Boulders means working with a locally owned, family-operated business that treats every project with care and detail. Unlike quarried stone, our engineered stone is versatile, durable, and fully customizable, allowing us to create designs that fit your vision.

From custom address rocks to commercial monuments, headstones, and ranch signage, we provide affordable options without sacrificing quality. Our stone decorations are not only long-lasting but are also recognized as beautifications, which can simplify approvals with municipalities.

With us, you get dependable service, creative solutions, and stonework from skilled custom stone fabricators that stand the test of time.

As your trusted custom stone designer, every project begins with the goal of creating something meaningful and enduring. For homeowners, we craft residential address rocks and decorative landscaping stone that add both charm and character to everyday spaces.

Businesses turn to us for commercial monuments and landmarks that reflect professionalism while standing strong for years to come. Families seeking affordable headstones find comfort in knowing we provide heartfelt, dignified tributes without the overwhelming cost of granite.

Why Choose an Engineered Stone Sign Over Other Signage?

  • Built to Last: Unlike plastic, wood, or metal signs that fade or warp, engineered stone endures decades of weather with minimal upkeep.
  • Unique Aesthetic: Each engineered stone sign is one-of-a-kind, custom-shaped and designed to suit your home, ranch, or business identity.
  • Permanent & Theft-Proof: No one’s walking away with your sign. And the wind won’t knock it down.
  • Customizable: Choose shapes, fonts, colors, and logos that match your brand or family style.
  • Eco-Friendly: No disposable materials, no landfill waste, and low lifetime maintenance.
