The Smart, Standout Choice for Long-Lasting Signage in Austin, TX

If you’re deciding between a traditional sign and something with real presence, here’s why more home and business owners across Austin, TX, are choosing custom-crafted engineered stones from Boulder Designs by Daulton Stoneworks.

Our engineered stones are custom-made, built to last for decades, and crafted to look like natural stone, without any of the limitations of real rock or traditional materials like wood, vinyl, or metal.

Low-Maintenance

Traditional signs break down fast. Paint chips. Metal rusts. Wood warps. Not ours.

Our engineered stones require no constant maintenance. They’re sealed to withstand Texas weather, and they keep their sharp look for years, without ever needing a touch-up.

Easy to See from the Street

A sign only works if people notice it.

We design every engineered stone with clear visibility in mind:

  • High-contrast lettering
  • Dimensional detailing for added depth
  • Bold shapes that stand out from the landscape
  • Whether it’s for your business, ranch, or home, your engineered stone makes a statement from the curb

Built to Last

Our stones are engineered to endure. They don’t fade, split, or buckle. They’re impact-resistant and weather-sealed to last for decades, not just seasons.

Invest once, and never worry about your sign again.

Any Shape. Any Size.

Need something tall and slim? Wide and low? Square, round, or custom? We build to fit your space.

Perfect for:

  • Business entrances.
  • Subdivision monuments.
  • Ranch gates.
  • Memorial and dedication stones.
  • Residential address markers.

An Earth-Friendly, Longer-Lasting Choice

When a sign lasts for decades, you don’t need to replace it every few years. That means less waste, fewer materials, and a much smaller environmental footprint.

Our engineered stones:

  • Require no electricity or lighting.
  • Don’t involve toxic finishes or coatings.
  • They are built to stay in place, not head to the landfill.
  • If sustainability matters to you, this is signage done right.

Why Not Just Use Stone?

Natural stone may seem like a solid option until you try working with it. Real rock is inconsistent, difficult to shape, and expensive to install. And once it’s placed, you can’t easily adjust or replace it.

Our engineered stones give you the look of natural stone, but with major advantages:

  • Fully customizable designs.
  • Greater control over finish and color.
  • Easier transport and installation.
  • Longer-lasting surface treatments.
  • And typically cost about half as much as real stone.
  • You get the strength and style of stone, without the headaches or the high price.
