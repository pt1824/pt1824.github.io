Why Engineered Stone?

At Bedrock Boulders, we specialize in crafting custom pieces from engineered stone that are built to last. Whether you're honoring a loved one, showcasing your ranch, or advertising your business, we’re here to deliver timeless, durable results.

Our engineered stone sets us apart. It’s not only cost-effective—allowing us to offer headstones starting at $899—but also highly durable, designed to withstand Texas’s extreme weather, from scorching summers to heavy storms. Unlike traditional materials, our stone resists cracking, fading, and erosion, ensuring your memorial or sign remains pristine for generations.

We combine cutting-edge design software with hands-on craftsmanship in our Houston workshop. From your initial concept to the final installation, most projects are completed in 6–10 weeks, making us one of the fastest in the industry without sacrificing attention to detail.
