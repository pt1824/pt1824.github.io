Personalizing Your Headstone: Symbols and Engravings That Tell a Story

Posted on April 5, 2025 | By Bedrock Boulders Team

A headstone is a canvas for telling a loved one’s story, and personalization makes it unique. At Bedrock Boulders, we specialize in custom engravings and symbols that capture individuality. Common choices include religious icons, such as crosses or stars, which reflect spiritual beliefs, or nature motifs like trees and flowers to symbolize peace. Military emblems or professional insignias can honor a career or service. For a more personal touch, consider incorporating a favorite quote, a significant date, or even a custom-designed logo that represents a hobby or passion.


Our engineered stone allows for precise, detailed engravings that remain sharp over time, even in Houston’s harsh weather. We work closely with families to translate their vision into a design that feels authentic, offering 3D renderings to preview the final product. Whether it’s a simple name and date or an elaborate tribute, personalization ensures the headstone resonates with meaning. Ready to create a one-of-a-kind memorial? Contact Bedrock Boulders to explore our customization options and start designing a headstone that tells your loved one’s story.
